Committee

To ensure that the best projects are selected for presentations at the conference, we have two separate committees.

The Scientific Committee reviews the submitted abstracts for the academic track. We strongly encourage junior researchers to submit their abstracts.


The Expert Committee reviews submitted data journalism projects. We have included experts from media research, data scientists, and data journalists to ensure that the best projects are presented at the conference.
