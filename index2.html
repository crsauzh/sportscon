SPORTS, DATA, & JOURNALISM

THE CRSA SAYS THANK YOU!





Researchers, data journalists, experts from media and industry met at the Sports, Data, and Journalism Conference on October 25, 2018

Data science, the sexiest job of the 21st century, has arrived in sports and is here to stay. The gathering and analyses of data in sports plays a key role for researchers, data intelligence and technology firms, sports organizations, and media institutions alike.


The Center for Research in Sports Administration (CRSA) organizes a conference on Sports, Data, and Journalism on Thursday, October 25, 2018. The conference provides a platform for experts from research, media, and industry to discuss the use of data science in sports, thereby demystifying its role in sports research.

Keynotes and Presentations

The conference hosts a keynote speech and numerous presentation sessions for academics, analysts, and data journalists. If you are interested in becoming a speaker at our conference, please submit your abstract. We will host presentations both from academics and practicians.

Workshops

Freshen up your data analytics skills, understand data visualization methods, or learn how to get started with Python. You will have the chance to attend several workshops on different topics in data science at the conference.

Discussions

Receive feedback on your own work, critically challenge research results, learn about new methods and exchange ideas on the use of data science in sports.

Location

The conference takes place at the University of Zurich, Rämistrasse 59, Building RAA.
