Call for Papers and Projects

The conference will host academic research and data driven journalism projects. Please make sure to submit your abstract for your preferred track. You can download our Call for Projects below.

Academic Track

We accept abstracts for research papers now. Please submit your abstract (max 1 page without references). Make sure to clearly outline the data and methods you have used, and your results. We strongly encourage junior researchers to submit their abstracts, as they will have the chance to present their research.


We will soon publish the call for papers for the academic track

Professionals Track

If you are an analyst, hacker, data journalist and love to work with sports data, then please hand in your project. Please submit your abstract (max 1 page without references). Our Expert Committee will review your projects and select the best ones for presentation at the conference.


We will soon publish the call for projects for the data driven journalism track
